We love medium and medium-heavy board games, with the occasional lighter game as a filler. Every meetup has a theme, and members bring games that fit that theme or its mechanics.

You **don't need to own any games to join us**. If you bring one, we simply ask that you're willing and well prepared to teach it to the people joining your table.
