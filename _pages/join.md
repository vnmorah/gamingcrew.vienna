#Do you want to play with us?

Our group organizes via Discord. To see the details of our next event and join our tables, you need to be a member.

Game Days are every two weeks, alternating Saturday/Sundays. We usually meet between 2:30 - 8:30 PM at a public location.

Request an invitation to become a member and join our events.
